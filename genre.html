Genre Page

Books By Genre

Fiction

Fiction is a genre of literature that involves imaginary events, characters, and settings.

Non-Fiction

Non-fiction is a genre of literature that involves factual information and real events.

Mystery

Mystery is a genre of literature that involves solving a puzzle or uncovering a hidden truth.

Science Fiction

Science fiction is a genre of literature that involves imaginative and futuristic concepts.

Romance

Romance is a genre of literature that involves romantic relationships and emotional journeys.

Humour

Humour refers to quality of being amusing,funny, or entertaining.Humour can take many forms including jokes,comedy,satire,and sarcasm.

Thriller

A Thriller is genre of literature,film or television characterized by suspendful and gripping storytelling.

Spiritual

The "spiritual" genre encompasses a range of media, including music, literature, and more, that explore religious, philosophical, and metaphysical themes..

History

This genre aims to educate and entertain the reader by chronicling a historical event.Historical fiction is a literary genre in which a fictional plot takes place in the setting of particular real historical events. Although the term is commonly used as a synonym for historical fiction literature

Education Non-Fiction

Books in the education nonfiction genre can either be books about forms of education and how it's achieved or be educational itself, in the form of text books and educational materials. The act of educating is to impart something in a learnable way to someone that does not yet have that knowledge
